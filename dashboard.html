Diagnostics Performance Dashboard

Preparation Instructions

To populate these graphs with your real on-device data:

  1. Download the diagnostics.csv from your iPhone (following the Validation Guide).
  2. Open the CSV and copy your values for FPS, Latency, and Memory.
  3. Open this dashboard.html file in a text editor.
  4. Replace the empty arrays in the <script> section at the bottom with your real numbers.
